Set up an authenticator app

  • Open your authenticator app on your smartphone, tablet or computer.
    • I don’t have an authenticator app

    You can download an authenticator app for your smartphone or tablet from your app store.

    If you don’t have a smartphone or tablet, search online for an authenticator app for your computer.

    You can use any authenticator app.

  • Use your authenticator app to scan the QR code or type the secret key into your authenticator app. Some authenticator apps call the secret key a 'code.

    • I cannot scan the QR code

    You can enter the secret key into your authenticator app instead.

    Secret key:
    UZ5T R7MN HXQD 5NSL HVIG HNW4 D47R OYGB

    Some authenticator apps call the secret key a 'code'.

  • The authenticator app will show a security code.
  • Enter the security code.

It might take a few minutes to arrive. The code will expire after 15 minutes.
