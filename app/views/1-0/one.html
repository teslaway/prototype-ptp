Sign in or create an account using GOV.UK One Login

You can create an account, or sign in if you already have an account. Once you have signed in successfully, you will be automatically redirected to your Prevent Training Portal.

To create a GOV.UK One Login account, or sign in, you'll need

  • an email address
  • a mobile phone

You’ll be given the opportunity to create a new GOV.UK One Login if you don’t already have one.
